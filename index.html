Dr.A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

The man behind development of India's missile and nuclear weapons

Here's a time line of Dr.Abdul Kalam's life:

  • 1931 - Born in Rameswaram,Tamil Nadu,India
  • 1958 - Earned a degree in Aeronautical Engineering . Joined in Defence Research and Development Organization.
  • 1969 -Moved to Indian Space Research Organization. Project director of SLV-3,the first launch satellite vehicle that was designed and produced in India.
  • 1982 - Produced a number of successful missiles,then nickname "Missile Man".
  • 1990 - Received Padma Vibhusan Award.
  • 1997 - Received Bharat Ratna.
  • 1998 - Nuclear weapon test and became a National Hero.
  • 1999 - Wrote "Wings of Fire".
  • 2002 - India's 11th president.
  • 2015 -Collapse with cardiac arrest.

“A scientist and statesman, Kalam rose from humble beginnings to become one of India’s most accomplished leaders, earning esteem at home and abroad,”

-- American President Obama
